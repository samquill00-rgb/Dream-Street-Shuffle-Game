DREAM STREET SHUFFLE

23.11.73 Dean Street, Soho, London.

A heavy afternoon lingers in rainfall on the pavements though the sky has cleared now and the newborn night is cut with moonshine.

There is an atmosphere of the silver screen: as if Brando might emerge in a tight and sodden T-shirt, from any door or alleyway, anytime.

In your bag, you have your novel in manuscript. You need to sell it, pronto.
