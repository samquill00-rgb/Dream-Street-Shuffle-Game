DREAM STREET SHUFFLE — DARK SUN PREVIEW

Two candidates for the Alba Incomplete reveal — the alchemical Sol Niger or an eclipse. Original Sol shown at the bottom for reference.
A — SOL NIGER (alchemical)
The blackened sun with a faint face and three-point crown — the nigredo, the corpse of the king at the start of the opus. No rays.
B — ECLIPSE
A total eclipse — dark disc with bright corona ring and a diamond-ring bead at one edge. Stars come out. Lilies stay warm: the natural world is itself.
